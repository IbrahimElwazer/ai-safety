About

This webpage was made as a final product of an intensive 8-weeks research project concerning the AI Control Problem, the fear of Artificial General Intelligence dominating and harming humanity in the future. The project team consists of University students from Finland and China, brought together by Demola, international innovation challenge platform based in Tampere, Finland. As a group of international University students, we believe that it is essential for us to contribute to the ongoing growing field of AI Satefy Solution, aiming at finding both theoretical and technical solutions for the longly-debated AI Control Problem.
